Data Science

Big Data Analysis, Statistical Modelling & Data‐Driven Storytelling

With a PhD in Astronomy and over 5 years driving big-data research, I specialize in designing scalable analytics pipelines, statistical inference, and interactive visualizations to turn complex datasets into actionable insights.

  • Collaborated across disciplines—atmospheric scientists, astrophysicists, and engineers—to adapt a 3D Earth‐system model (CESM2-WACCM6) for exoplanet simulations, managing ETL pipelines for over 1 PB of structured climate data
  • Designed and executed 100+ controlled experiments on HPC clusters; extracted and transformed data with SQL and Python to support hypothesis‐driven testing.
  • Developed Python-based data pipelines and visualizations using Pandas, Xarray, and Matplotlib in Jupyter; presented findings biannually at international venues such as the NASA Sagan Workshop and Rocky Worlds III
  • Applied machine learning techniques for classification and regression on climate stability metrics, achieving robust predictive performance.
  • Version-controlled all analysis code on GitHub and published scientifically validated datasets to Dryad for community reuse (doi:10.5061/dryad.jsxksn0f5, doi:10.5061/dryad.2fqz612x7)
